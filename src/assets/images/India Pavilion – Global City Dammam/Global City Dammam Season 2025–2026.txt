Global City Dammam Season 2025–2026
Shams Al Barakat Exhibitions was proud to be entrusted with the organization and execution of the India Pavilion and Africa Pavilion for the inaugural season of Global City Dammam 2025–2026.

From concept to completion, our team successfully managed every stage of the project with precision and professionalism. Our scope of work included the complete design and construction of both pavilions, vendor sourcing and onboarding for retail spaces, as well as comprehensive planning, coordination, and on-site project management.

By combining creativity, technical expertise, and seamless execution, we transformed the vision into vibrant cultural destinations that showcased authentic experiences, shopping, cuisine, and entertainment. Every aspect of the project was carefully planned and delivered to the highest standards, ensuring a smooth and successful operation throughout the season.

This milestone reflects our commitment to delivering large-scale exhibitions and events with excellence, innovation, and meticulous attention to detail, reinforcing our reputation as a trusted partner for world-class event management and exhibition solutions
